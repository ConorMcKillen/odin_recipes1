Irish Spice Bag

Description

An Irish culinary gem, the spice bag is the ultimate late-night takeaway.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 large potatoes, cut into fries
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 red chillies, finely sliced (adjust based on spice preference)
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp Chinese five-spice powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Begin by heating the oil in a large frying pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. While the oil heats, in a large bowl, combine the chicken pieces with half of the paprika, cumin, Chinese five-spice, salt, and pepper. Mix well, ensuring each piece is coated with the spices.
  3. Once the oil is hot, add the chicken pieces, frying until they are golden and crispy. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  4. In the same oil, fry the potato fries until they're golden and crispy. Once done, remove and set them aside with the chicken.
  5. In the remaining oil, saut√© the garlic, onions, bell peppers, and chillies until they're soft and fragrant.
  6. Add the fried chicken and chips back into the wok or pan. Sprinkle over the remaining spices and toss everything together, ensuring the chicken and chips are well-coated with the spices and the vegetables are evenly distributed.
  7. Serve hot, ideally with some dipping sauces on the side, like sweet chili sauce or garlic mayo.
