Beans on Toast

Description

Beans and Toast is the classic

Ingredients

  • Tin of beans
  • Two slices of bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Start by preparing the toast. Place the slices of bread in a toaster or on a grill, toasting them until they are golden brown.
  2. While the bread is toasting, pour the beans into a saucepan and heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally. If you prefer a richer taste, you can add a dollop of butter to the beans as they cook.
  3. Once the toast is ready, spread a generous amount of butter over each slice.
  4. Spoon the hot beans over the buttered toast, ensuring an even distribution.
  5. Optionally, season with salt, pepper, or a dash of hot sauce to taste.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Beans on Toast!
